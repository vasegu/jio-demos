The Problem
Jio reacts to complaints after they happen. We're building a system to act before—preventing churn, seizing cross-sell moments, and deflecting contact centre calls through proactive signals.
The Solution: Jio Buddy
A Proactive Voice Agent
Knows everything that's happened to a customer—even when they weren't talking. Acts across network issues, travel moments, billing confusion, and loyalty milestones.
Timeline: Live in 2 regions by June 30, 2026. NPS + CX lift measured.
How We Work
We're finding SIGNALS — measurable events in data that trigger Jio Buddy to act. Each signal answers five questions: What is it? How far ahead? Why act? Live or deferred? What does the agent do?
