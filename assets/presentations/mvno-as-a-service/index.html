MVNO-as-a-Service

The Connectivity Engine Behind Every Brand

Enable any brand, team, or artist to offer white-label mobile services on India's largest 4G/5G network. Jio handles the telecom complexity — partners own the customer relationship.

Overview

Jio as the Invisible Network Layer

Jio MVNO-as-a-Service positions Reliance Jio as the connectivity engine behind fan, brand, and influencer mobile services across India. Brands own the customer relationship, the UX, and the commercial proposition — Jio provides the network, the platform, and the regulatory backbone.

This mirrors the global embedded mobile model pioneered by platforms like Gigs OS, which enables Revolut to offer in-app mobile plans on Vodafone's network in the UK. Jio's version is purpose-built for India's regulatory environment (the VNO framework under the Telecommunications Act 2023), its scale (500M+ subscribers, 234M+ 5G users), and its unique ecosystem advantages (JioCinema, JioFiber, JioMart).

Service Offering

What Jio Provides to Partners

01

Network Access

Full 4G/5G mobile network access across India. Optionally bundled with JioFiber for home/office packages. 99.9% urban population coverage.

02

White-Label OSS/BSS

Multi-tenant activation, number management, billing, payments, and care tooling — all configurable per partner brand. Partners see their brand; subscribers see the partner. Jio is invisible.

03

eSIM/SIM Provisioning

APIs for in-app eSIM activation without store visits. Physical SIM fulfilment where needed. MNP integration so subscribers keep their existing numbers.

04

Compliance & KYC

Full regulatory handling under India's VNO framework and the Telecommunications Act 2023. Digital eKYC via Aadhaar, TRAI compliance, lawful intercept, and security controls — all managed by Jio.

05

Analytics & Segmentation

Engagement dashboards, ARPU tracking, churn prediction, and fan segmentation tools — shared with partners to optimise offers, content, and retention.

Roadmap

Scaling the Platform

Launch with 1-2 flagship partners (sports franchise + top-tier artist). Validate the platform, refine onboarding, demonstrate unit economics.
