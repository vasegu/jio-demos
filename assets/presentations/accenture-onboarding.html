Context

Who is Jio, and what is the gap?

To understand what we are building, you first need to understand the scale of Jio — and the distance between what it knows about its customers and what it does with that knowledge.

The company
Reliance Jio Infocomm Ltd.
India's largest telecom operator, launched in 2016. "Jio" means "live long" in Hindi. Built on the premise that every Indian deserves fast, affordable digital connectivity.
The platform
A super-app, not just a network
True5G and JioFiber are the backbone. Jio has expanded into entertainment, payments, commerce, and cloud — all unified in the MyJio super-app.
True5G JioFiber JioCinema JioSaavn JioMart JioFinance UPI MyJio
The scale
500 million signals, unused
Every subscriber generates network events, billing patterns, usage behaviour, and expressed intent. This data is captured. It is not yet used to act proactively. That is the gap we are closing.
Today: Reactive CX
When a customer has a problem, they call. Or they silently churn. Jio responds after the fact, when the cost has already been incurred.
Missed moments
A customer flies to Singapore — Jio doesn't know until the bill spikes. Network drops for three days before a port-out — Jio could have acted on day one. IPL is tonight — nobody configured the bandwidth.
Contact centre cost
Millions of inbound calls every month for problems that were detectable in advance. Every call Jio Buddy prevents is a direct cost saving and an NPS point gained.
The opportunity
The signals are already in the data. Network degradation precedes churn by 72 hours. Travel intent precedes the roaming bill by days. We need an agent that reads them and acts.

The vision

Jio Buddy

A proactive voice agent that acts on customer signals across the full CX lifecycle: churn prevention, cross-sell, experience improvement, contact deflection.

The stack

Signal Layer
Flink + Kafka — real-time streams of network, billing, behavioural, and contextual data
Memory Store
Firestore + Databricks — customer context, signal history, preference state
Agent
Gemini 2.5 Flash + ADK — decision logic, Jio Buddy persona, response synthesis
Voice
Gemini Live API + IndicWhisper — natural conversation in Indian languages
