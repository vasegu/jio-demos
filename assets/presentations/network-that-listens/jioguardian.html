Safety Wrapped Around Your Family at the Network Level

Network-level protection that works across every app, every browser, every device — without installing anything.

Protect Your Family Now
JioGuardian Mesh moves online safety from the device to the network itself — protecting every connected device in your family through Jio's core infrastructure, not through apps that can be uninstalled or bypassed.

Traditional parental controls and security apps have a fundamental weakness — they live on the device. A child can delete an app, switch browsers, or use a friend's phone, and the protection vanishes. JioGuardian Mesh operates at a fundamentally different level. It is built into Jio's 5G core network, meaning protection applies to every DNS request, every connection, and every data flow from any tagged device — regardless of which app or browser is being used.

Parents designate "Guardian" and "Protected" devices through MyJio. Protected devices automatically receive content filtering, threat blocking, anomaly detection, and SOS call priority routing. Guardian devices receive alerts and control access to settings. Because the filtering happens at the network policy layer, it cannot be bypassed by switching apps, using incognito mode, or connecting to a different Wi-Fi network while on Jio.

How It Works

Three steps to network-level family protection

Guardian Mesh turns Jio's 5G core into a safety layer that surrounds your family — no apps to install, no settings to maintain on every device.

1

Tag Your Devices

Open JioGuardian Mesh in MyJio. Designate which devices are "Guardian" (parents) and which are "Protected" (children or vulnerable family members). Each protected device gets a network-level security profile.

2

Network-Level Filtering

Jio's core network applies real-time content filtering, threat detection, and anomaly monitoring to all traffic from protected devices. Risky websites, phishing attempts, and known threat domains are blocked before they reach the device.

3

Alerts and SOS Priority

Guardians receive instant alerts when a protected device encounters a blocked threat or when usage patterns change unusually. SOS calls from protected devices are routed through a priority lane for faster connection to emergency services.

Features

Protection that lives in the network, not on the device

Network-Level Blocking

Risky sites, phishing domains, and malware sources are blocked at the Jio network core — not on the device. Works across all apps and browsers, including incognito modes.

Anomaly Detection

AI monitors usage patterns and alerts guardians to unusual activity — sudden spikes in data, access attempts to blocked categories, or connectivity from unexpected locations.

SOS Priority Routing

Emergency calls from protected devices are placed in a priority queue on the network, reducing connection time to emergency services by routing through a dedicated slice.

Age-Appropriate Profiles

Pre-configured protection levels for different age groups. A profile for a 7-year-old filters differently from one for a 15-year-old, and both differ from an elderly family member's profile.
