Your Personal Lanes on the Jio Network

Prioritise what matters — play, work, study or safety — for every device in your home, in a single tap.

Set Up Your Lanes in MyJio
JioSlices takes the power of 5G network slicing — a technology once reserved for enterprises and industrial systems — and places it directly in your hands. Think of it as personal lanes on the Jio highway, each one shaped around a moment that matters to you: a child studying for board exams, a parent on a critical video call, or the whole family gathered for a cricket final.

Today, every device in your home shares the same undifferentiated connection. Your son's online class competes with your daughter's Instagram reels, your work laptop fights for bandwidth with the smart TV, and nobody wins. The result is buffering at the worst possible moment, frozen video calls when the stakes are highest, and a creeping sense that your connection should be smarter than this.

JioSlices changes the equation entirely. Through Jio's 5G standalone core, each device on your account can be assigned its own prioritised lane — directly from the MyJio app. A parent can set "Study" priority on a child's tablet during homework hours, "Work" priority on a laptop before a client presentation, and "Play" priority on the television for match night. The network adapts in real time, allocating bandwidth, tuning latency, and optimising reliability for each device independently.

There are no routers to configure, no QoS settings to learn, and no technical knowledge required. You simply choose a lane, and the Jio network does the heavy lifting — provisioning a dedicated slice on the 5G standalone infrastructure, invisible to you but profoundly felt in the quality of every stream, every call, and every download.

How It Works

Three steps to a smarter home network

No hardware, no configuration screens, no waiting. JioSlices works entirely through the MyJio app and the Jio 5G standalone core.

1

Open MyJio

Tap the JioSlices panel in your MyJio app. Every Jio device on your account appears automatically — phones, tablets, laptops, connected TVs — all listed and ready to manage.

2

Assign a Lane

Choose a priority mode for each device: Play for low-latency gaming and streaming, Work for stable video calls and uploads, Study for consistent bandwidth during online classes, or Safety for emergency-priority connectivity.

3

The Network Adapts

Jio's 5G standalone core instantly provisions a dedicated network slice for that device. Bandwidth, latency, and reliability are optimised in real time, and revert automatically when the moment passes.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

JioSlices is designed to be powerful under the hood and effortless on the surface. Here is what you get.

Per-Device Control

Assign different priorities to different devices so your child's phone never competes with your work laptop. Each device gets its own slice of the network, tuned to exactly what it needs in the moment.

Scheduled Lanes

Set lanes to activate automatically on a recurring schedule. Put the kids' devices into Study mode every weekday from four to seven in the evening, and let the network handle the rest without a second thought.

Real-Time Switching

Change lanes mid-session without drops or reconnections. The network slice reconfigures in under a second, so you can shift from Work to Play the instant your meeting ends — seamlessly and instantly.

Family Dashboard

See which devices are on which lane, how much bandwidth each is consuming, and when lanes were last changed. A single, clear view of your household's network — designed for parents, not network engineers.
