# H2 — Silent Sufferer Outreach

## What It Does
Identifies customers with terrible QoS metrics who never complain. Reach out proactively to offer support or credit before they churn silently.

## Why It Works
- Silent sufferers have 3.4x churn rate vs. complainers
- No signal in customer contact data — only visible in network metrics
- Proactive outreach interrupts silent exit

## Success Metric
- Identify 95% of silent sufferers in test cohort
- Reach rate >70% (voice + SMS)
- Engagement lift: 40%+ responding to outreach
- 2-week retention lift vs. control cohort
