# H1 — Proactive Credit

## What It Does
When network SINR + competitor proximity signal an imminent port-out (72h ahead), reach out proactively with a credit offer before the customer complains.

## Why It Works
- Detects churn precursor signal 3 days in advance (vs. 0 days with reactive care)
- Interrupts the decision journey before customer calls competitor
- Personalized: offer amount tied to customer LTV + churn risk

## Success Metric
- Prevent 5-10% of predicted port-outs in test cohort
- Positive NPS lift on reached segment
- Offer acceptance rate >20%
